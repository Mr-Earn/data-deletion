Data Deletion Request – Mr Earn

App Name: Mr Earn
Developer: Mr Earn

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to request deletion of your personal data stored by the Mr Earn app, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at support@mrearnapp.com with the subject: "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the following details in your email:
    • Your registered email address or username
    • Your phone number (if associated with your account)
    • Any payment details used for withdrawals (e.g., PayPal, bank info)
  3. We will verify your identity and process your data deletion request within 7 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Email address and username
  • Phone number
  • Payment details (e.g., PayPal, bank account info)
  • Earnings and transaction history
  • Team and activity data
  • App usage preferences and settings

Data That May Be Retained

  • Fraud & Compliance Logs: Retained for up to 90 days to meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Anonymous Usage Analytics: May be kept (non-identifiable) to improve app performance

If you have any concerns or questions, contact our support team at: mrearnapps@gmail.com
